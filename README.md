📌 Project Overview

Understanding customer purchasing behavior is essential for improving business performance in the retail industry. This project analyzes over 3,900 customer transaction records to identify purchasing trends, customer segments, product performance, and promotional effectiveness.

The project explores:

Which customer segments generate the highest revenue
How subscription status influences purchasing behavior
Which product categories drive the highest sales
Whether discounts significantly impact customer purchases
How shipping methods affect purchase value
Which demographic groups should be targeted for marketing campaigns

💡 Key Business Insights
🎯 Young Adults Drive Maximum Revenue

The Young Adult customer segment contributes the highest revenue, making it the ideal target for future marketing campaigns.

🚚 Express Shipping Customers Spend More

Customers choosing Express Shipping consistently record higher average purchase values than other shipping methods.

💰 Promotions Drive Significant Sales

Several product categories rely on discounts for over 50% of their total sales, indicating strong price sensitivity.

⭐ Subscription Opportunity

A large percentage of repeat customers continue purchasing without subscribing, revealing an opportunity to improve customer loyalty and subscription conversion.

👕 Clothing Category Leads Sales

The Clothing category contributes the highest revenue and sales volume, making it the strongest-performing product segment.
