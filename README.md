The solution transforms raw transactional data into an interactive executive dashboard that helps businesses optimize marketing strategies, improve customer retention, and maximize revenue.

## 📌 Project Overview

Retail businesses generate massive amounts of customer transaction data, but turning that data into actionable business decisions remains a challenge.

This project analyzes **3,900+ customer transaction records** to identify purchasing patterns, customer demographics, promotional effectiveness, and revenue-driving factors that help businesses make informed strategic decisions.

This project explores:

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue
- How subscription status influences purchasing behavior
- Which product categories drive the most sales
- How discounts impact purchasing decisions
- Which shipping methods increase customer spending
- How businesses can improve customer retention and marketing strategies

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### 🎯 Young Adults Generate the Highest Revenue

The **Young Adult** customer segment contributes the highest revenue, making it the ideal audience for targeted marketing campaigns.

---

### 🚚 Express Shipping Customers Spend More

Customers selecting **Express Shipping** consistently generate a higher average purchase value compared to other shipping methods.

---

### 💰 Promotions Drive Product Sales

Several product categories depend on promotional discounts for more than **50% of total sales volume**, indicating strong pricing sensitivity.

---

### ⭐ Subscription Opportunity

A significant percentage of repeat customers continue purchasing without subscribing, revealing an opportunity to improve subscription conversion strategies.

---

### 👕 Clothing Category Leads Revenue

The Clothing category contributes the highest sales volume and revenue, making it the strongest-performing product segment.

## 🧹 Data Preparation

- Cleaned and standardized column names
- Converted columns into snake_case format
- Implemented category-specific median imputation for missing review ratings
- Created demographic age groups using Quantile Binning (`pd.qcut`)
- Converted purchase frequency into numerical intervals
- Exported processed data directly into MySQL
